OARS

  [ɔːr]
Noun

OARS is the abbreviation for Online Activity Registration System. It is developed by a group of five students from the Faculty of Computer Science & Information Technology, University of Malaya that provides below functionalities :

  1. Allow students to view and register for any events hosted by any residential college.
  2. Allow students to make a complaint should they found any problems in the residential college.
  3. Allow students to order food served by all cafeterias in residential college.

About Us

Online activity registration system (a.k.a OARS) allows students to discover and register for popular events in residential colleges of University of Malaya (UM). Besides, students are able to report an issue should they found at residential college and order food from the cafeterias using OARS.
